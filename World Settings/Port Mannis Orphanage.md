
Minor Plot Setting for the story
The local orphanage of Port Mannis

Being the only orphanage in Port Mannis, it is one of the biggest buildings in town. Here, all the orphans are housed, waiting to eventually find their own home. Despite it's welcoming appearance from the outside, the mothers of the Orphanage hide their secret as human traffickers.

Melisa Alice, the head superintendent of the orphanage is one of the eight council members of The Cult. She uses the cult as both a way to continue her underground business of human trafficking while indoctrinating children into The Cult.
