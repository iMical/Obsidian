
Secondary plot setting for the story
The forest that borders Port Mannis.

Green Vine Forest is a forest. Known for its unique species of bees and flowers, resulting in Port Mannis' second most profitable source of income, honey. In addition to its honey production, Green Vine Forest is the home of the world's rarest snake species, the Green Vine snake.

The forest itself spans nearly double the size of Port Mannis, creating a quiet, natural wall that concealed Port Mannis prior to its urbanization efforts. Now, the many tops of Port Mannis' buildings peak over the forest, a way to show humanity's power in knowledge.

Due to the size of the forest, the world is unsure of the secrets that hide within. Throughout Sarah Hills' investigation, she discovers that the Green Vine Forest is home to much more than snakes that hide among the trees.
