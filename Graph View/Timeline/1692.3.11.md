
Sarah Hills sends Kenzie Thomson a letter. She wants to meet, hoping to get more clues as to John Smith's accusation. Kenzie Thomson responds, no longer wanting to help Sarah Hills, a stark difference mysteriously formed in a week. She gives Sarah Hills one last hint, Green Vine Forest.

Gary Walker sends a letter to Kenzie Thomson, telling her that she is cursed. He tells Kenzie to follow John Smith and not Sarah Hills.
