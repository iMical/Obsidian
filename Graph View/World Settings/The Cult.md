
Main plot setting for the story
The home base for The Cult

Within the Green Vine Forest, lays the hidden underground building of The Cult. Here resides most if not all members of The Cult, whom blend in perfectly within the citizens of Port Mannis. They have connections throughout the town as a whole, holding political strength similar, if not stronger than the mayor, Tim Burghton.

The building spans a total of 5 floors underground. Its origins are unknown as it would be near impossible for humans to build using current world technological advancements. The building gives off an eerie vibe, getting darker and more gothic as visitors travel down. from the surface, the building seems to be a random, run down cabin that existed long before the coming of Port Mannis.
