
Minor Plot setting for the story
Located within Port Mannis, fixing and building structures

Port Mannis Construction Corp is the only construction company, credit for the upbringing and withholding of Port Mannis. Being such a small town, it is a silent agreement amongst the citizens of Port Mannis that another construction company is simply not needed, rather, a nuisance towards the preexisting company.

Ran by owner, Brad Hernandez, PMCC prioritizes speed and quality over quantity, highlighting the fact that Port Mannis is quite small for its extravagant buildings that have stood for generations prior to its current time.

There has been little to no failures in PMCC's buildings except for the burning of Port Mannis Pub, showing their dedication to the work. They are also exposed at the end of the story for being the creator of the "homebase" in Green Vine Forest, where The Cult lives.
